Food Delivery service

Project Background

Fuya is a cross platform mobile applications which allows its users to order food from their favourite local resturant or food vendors. The aim of Fuya as an entity is to enable local food vendors who are locked out of the market to enter whilst boosting the economy in townships .

Challenges & Requirements

The technology-oriented start-ups are growing all over the world with the cutting edge technologies. As smartphones are widely used among the users, many development companies are investing more money into mobile app development to take the advantages of the growing demand for Apps. The world of the mobile app has seen substantial growth in the recent past. Thus, many entrepreneurs are planning to build mobile apps for their business keeping as the top of their business strategies. Understanding the challenges and creating a strategic solution to overcome them is very important. Below are some of the challenges during the development of Fuya

  • Validating and Evaluating user needs.
  • Choosing development technology.
  • Compatibility with different OS versions.
  • Security.

Actions & Outcomes

Each app is different and software development methodologies are always evolving, but there is a fairly standard process when developing mobile apps. This mobile app development process typically includes idea, strategy, design, development, deployment, and post-launch phases. Below are the steps followed abiding to the Agile software development methodology

  • Phase 1: Requirements.
  • Phase 2: Design.
  • Phase 3. Development and Coding.
  • Phase 4. Integration and Testing.
  • Phase 5. Implementation and Deployment.
  • Phase 6. Review.

Part of what helps ensure a smooth transition and exact implementation is the proper use of the available tools.
